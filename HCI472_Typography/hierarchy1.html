Hierarchy 1: Two Typefaces

This hierarchy uses two typefaces, Playfair Display for headings and Roboto for body text. The modular scale is based on a ratio of 1.25, creating a clear visual hierarchy while maintaining readability.

Heading 2

The Playfair Display typeface adds elegance to the headings, while Roboto ensures that the body text is clean and easy to read. This combination creates a balanced and visually appealing hierarchy for web content.
